What is
Find Me Football?

Search Game

Find Me Football is the perfect site for you if you are looking to play a game of football. We provide free games which anyone can join.
Anyone can play no matter age, gender, commitment etc. We provide games in local parks so that there is no fee needed. All you need to do is either turn up to the organised games at the date and time provided. or sign up to our newsfeed to receive emails about the special catagory games. Example of special games include, under 11's, 18 - 30, over 40's, Womens games and many more! Just sign up to our special games newsfeed.


Search Team

Or are you looking to join a local football but don't know how, who or where to go to? We will find local football teams looking for players and get back to you to organise a trial with the team. All you need to do is register your interest with us on our sign up page, leave your contact details and we will get back to you with clubs near you looking for players.


Or checkout our social media platforms at the bottom of the page. See if someone needs players for a game near you!
