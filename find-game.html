Find Me Football

Find Me A Game

We have weekly games, Doens't matter your age, gender, fitness lever, skill etc. We have a friendly community who accept everyone who wants to play the beautiful game.

When?

We play every Monday, Wednesday and Friday at 8:00pm and Saturday & Sunday 10:00am and 5:00pm.

Where?

We have 2 locations in Manchester - Chorlton Park, Heaton Park. You will recongise us at the park with our Find-Me-Football bibs.

How?

Our games are free, so all you need do, is Sign Up your interest 24 hours before kickoff for numbers. Turn up and have some fun.
